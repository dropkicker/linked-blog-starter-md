



###Autorecon
If you hate typing those long commands, most OSCP students add an **alias** to their `~/.zshrc` or `~/.bashrc` file:

Bash
```
alias sautorecon='sudo env "PATH=$PATH" autorecon'
```

After saving and running `source ~/.zshrc`, you can just type `sautorecon` to start your scans with full privileges.
